Master Grok Pro Extension

Chrome Web Store Extension — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Master Grok Pro is a Chrome extension that automates video generation on grok.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Master Grok Pro collects only the minimum information necessary to provide its service:

  • Extension Settings: Your preferences (language, default mode, delay settings) are stored locally in Chrome's built-in storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device only.
  • Prompt Queue: Your prompt queue is stored locally in Chrome's storage and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Usage Statistics: Daily usage count (prompts used today) is stored locally to enforce the free plan limit.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal information unless you voluntarily provide it for support.
  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not collect the content of your prompts or generated videos.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking scripts.

3. How the Extension Works

Master Grok Pro operates entirely within your browser. It:

  • Injects automation scripts into grok.com to type prompts and click buttons on your behalf
  • Downloads generated videos directly to your device via Chrome's download API
  • Stores all settings and queues locally — no data is sent to our servers

4. Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with grok.com (xAI) as part of its core functionality. Your use of grok.com is subject to xAI's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

For paid subscriptions, payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. We do not store your payment information. Please refer to Lemon Squeezy's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Permissions Used

  • tabs: To detect and interact with the active Grok tab
  • storage: To save your settings and prompt queue locally
  • downloads: To save generated videos to your computer
  • scripting: To inject automation scripts into grok.com
  • sidePanel: To display the extension as a side panel

6. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. No data is transmitted over the internet except when interacting with grok.com as part of normal extension operation.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
